Our Awesome Services.

Developing and prototyping ideas into reality has always been at the core of who we are. Our journey began humbly with a 1:1 restoration of a high-craftsmanship air conditioning panel for the iconic classic car. The success of that first project marked the start of something extraordinary. Since then, we've expanded our expertise to include interior and exterior lighting solutions, LCD restoration services, sticker artwork design, repair kits, and custom 3D product creation. Today, we proudly serve customers across Malaysia, collaborating with multiple strategic partners and manufacturers to deliver exceptional quality and services that set new benchmarks within the automotive industry.

Lighting Solutions.

We specialize in providing high-quality automotive lighting solutions designed to enhance both the functionality and aesthetics of your vehicle. From interior ambient lighting to exterior illumination upgrades, our solutions ensure superior performance, energy efficiency, and a modern look

LCD Restoration.

We offer professional LCD restoration services to bring faded, damaged, or malfunctioning automotive displays back to life. We use precise techniques and quality components to ensure your display looks and functions like new. Trust us to rejuvenate your vehicle’s LCD for a seamless and modern driving experience.

Sticker Artwork Design.

Transform your vehicle with our custom sticker artwork design services. Whether you’re looking for unique decals, branding, or personalized designs, we bring your ideas to life with precision and creativity. Using high-quality materials and expert craftsmanship, our stickers are durable, vibrant, and tailored to make your vehicle stand out on the road.

3D Design Solution.

Bring your concepts to life with our custom 3D design solutions. From prototyping unique automotive parts to creating bespoke accessories, we use cutting-edge technology to craft designs with precision and functionality. Whether for restoration, modification, or innovation, our 3D design services are tailored to meet your exact specifications, ensuring quality and creativity at every step.

Electronics Repair Kits.

Our electronics repair kits are designed to provide reliable solutions for fixing and restoring automotive electronic components. Equipped with high-quality parts, our kits are tailored to address common issues such as circuit failures, damaged connectors, and more. Whether you're a professional or a DIY enthusiast, our repair kits offer precision and durability to keep your vehicle’s electronics in top condition.

Parts Repair Kits.

Our parts repair kits provide comprehensive solutions for restoring and maintaining a variety of automotive components. Designed for durability and ease of use, these kits include high-quality tools and replacement parts to address common wear and tear. Whether it’s repairing mechanical components or interior features, our kits empower you to perform efficient and reliable repairs with confidence.
